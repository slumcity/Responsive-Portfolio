About Me

I've worked in the Real Estate Investment industry for the last 7 years, as well as starting my own business in 2012. I buy, renovate and sell homes in the Atlanta area, as well as work as a Regional Better Living Manager at Venterra Realty. My goal is to become a fullstack developer with the intention of developing a few of my own personal ideas I have for some proprietary mobile and web applications. When I'm not working or in school, I enjoy playing guitar and producing music for fun. I'm also really into sports, both watching and playing.
